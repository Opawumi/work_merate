Supply Chain Management That Keeps Operations Moving

WorkMerate's Supply Chain Management suite connects inventory, manufacturing, maintenance, quality, and AI-driven production intelligence into one unified system —helping organizations operate efficiently, reduce waste, and respond faster to demand.

Explore Capabilities Request a Demo

End-to-End Control Across Your Supply Chain

Supply chain efficiency depends on visibility, coordination, and timely decision-making. Disconnected inventory systems, manual production tracking, and reactive maintenance often lead to delays, waste, and lost revenue. WorkMerate centralizes supply chain operations into a single, integrated environment—connecting inventory movement, production planning, equipment maintenance, and quality control in real time.

  • Maintain accurate inventory levels across all locations
  • Optimize manufacturing operations and production planning
  • Reduce downtime through proactive maintenance
  • Ensure consistent product quality and compliance

Core Supply Chain Capabilities

Integrated modules delivering complete supply chain control from inventory to quality

Inventory

Inventory platform offers visibility, automated replenishment, and stock control.

  • Real-time inventory tracking with bin-level precision.
  • Stock movement orchestration for receipts and transfers.
  • Centralized visibility for multi-location warehouses.
  • Smart reordering engine with automated requisitions.

Manufacturing

Management system for manufacturing—offering control and cost insights.

  • BOMs for assemblies ensure accuracy.
  • Production order management includes tracking.
  • Capacity planning assesses machine bottlenecks.
  • Track costs: materials, labor, machine time, and profit.

AI Manufacturing

AI platform analyzes data, identifies optimizations, and provides insights to improve manufacturing.

  • Demand-driven production analyzes sales and inventory.
  • Analyze production flows to find constraints and optimize.
  • Forecast demand, model capacity.
  • Track KPIs, benchmark performance, identify trends.

Maintenance

Proactive platform turns maintenance into management—maximizing uptime.

  • Schedule maintenance with calendar, meter, and triggers.
  • Track equipment with specs, warranty, and location.
  • Track downtime, repair history, and costs with trends.
  • Track maintenance costs and downtime impact.

Quality

QA system prevents defects, ensures compliance, and drives improvement.

  • Quality check framework with templates for verification.
  • Track defects and supplier issues with alerts.
  • 5 Whys and fishbone diagrams improve quality.
  • Manage compliance docs, quality records, and audit trails.
