Tax Management Built for Compliance and Confidence

WorkMerate's Tax Management suite simplifies complex tax processes by automating calculations, tracking liabilities, and generating compliant reports—helping organizations stay accurate, transparent, and audit-ready at all times.

Explore Capabilities Request a Demo

Turn Complex Tax Obligations into Structured Processes

Tax management is one of the most sensitive areas of business operations—errors can lead to penalties, compliance risks, and reputational damage. WorkMerate centralizes tax computation, tracking, and reporting into a single, reliable system that aligns with your financial data. By integrating tax processes directly with accounting, payroll, and financial transactions, organizations can reduce manual calculations, improve accuracy, and remain compliant with evolving tax regulations.

  • Automate tax calculations and deductions
  • Maintain compliance with statutory requirements
  • Track liabilities and payment obligations proactively
  • Generate accurate, audit-ready tax reports

Core Tax Capabilities

Integrated modules ensuring complete tax compliance and strategic optimization

Income Tax

Automated tax engine for personal income tax with multi-jurisdiction support.

  • Automated tax computation with configurable brackets.
  • Dynamic tax bracket configuration with updates.
  • Integration with payroll for accurate tax tracking.
  • Tax summaries with year-to-date and updated projections.

Company Income Tax (CIT)

Tax management platform that automates profit-based tax obligations, ensuring compliance with local and federal rules.

  • Profit tax computation based on EBITDA and deferrals.
  • Engine for deprecation and credits.
  • Integration with statutory and GAAP-aligned tax rules.
  • Tax tracking with advance payments, and carryforward.

Capital Allowance

Tax optimization platform maximizing deductions and ensuring compliance through automation.

  • Asset categorization for depreciation and allowance.
  • Methods for straight-line, declining, and accelerated.
  • Integration with asset registers and ledger consistency.
  • Track allowances with audits and tax impact analysis.

Capital Gains Tax

Revenue ops connects commercial and financial systems for accurate revenue.

  • Track asset disposal with cost basis and transaction allocation.
  • Compute gain/loss using FIFO/LIFO and partial disposals.
  • Support short/long-term classifications and rates.
  • Automate tax reporting for Schedule D and state payments.

VAT Returns

VAT compliance platform for accurate tax calculation and error-free returns.

  • Real-time VAT tracking with automatic posting.
  • VAT management for exempt and zero-rated supplies.
  • Automated VAT return generation and filing.
  • Sales and purchase integration ensures VAT validation.

McFlow

Predictive tax platform offers visibility into obligations for proactive liquidity management.

  • Tax forecasting with payments and deadlines.
  • Smart payment schedules with reminders and management.
  • Cash flow analysis modeling payment scenarios.
  • Integrate accounting systems for automated payments.

Tax Reports

Tax platform for audit-grade reports and strategic analytics.

  • Tax summaries for visibility across income and VAT.
  • Period-based reporting for monthly and quarterly cycles.
  • Audit-ready documentation with transaction lineage.
  • Export options: PDF, Excel, XBRL, and formats for submission.
