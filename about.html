About WorkMerate

Automating taxes, streamlining workflows, and keeping Nigerian SMEs fully compliant with the Nigeria Tax Act 2025.

Our Identity

WorkMerate is an ERP platform built for Nigerian SMEs, designed to make tax compliance and daily operations simple, accurate, and stress-free. We designed the system to eliminate the inefficiencies of manual workflows, helping businesses automate tax records, streamline processes, and stay fully compliant without the hassle.

The Mission

To empower Nigerian SMEs to run smarter, compliant, and efficient businesses where tax compliance and workflow management are simple, accurate, and stress-free.

The Vision

To help SMEs automate tax records, streamline workflows, and exchange data seamlessly across all tax authorities so businesses can focus on growth, not paperwork.
